About US


Our goal: Provide you the best pet-adoption experience and pet-related supplies!

Pet Adoption

We have co-operated with SPCA and other pets organization for many years. Not all dogs and cats can have a stable home, and we keen to find a responsible owner for the wandering animals. What we hope is somehow easy but difficult to come true, so we need your help.

In our Station, there are many lovely animals that still can't find their home. If you want to keep a pet, instead of buying a dog, why not consider adoption? There are some advantages for adopting pets:

  • You can choose more types of cats/dogs with a nice characteristic ~
  • Animals' health can be more guarantee!
  • Save money, as you can enjoy many discounts offered by different pets organizations $$
  • Give an opportunity to wandering dogs/cats. You save a life!
  • Gain lots of supports and training services from us
  • Most important, in term of cuteness, there is no difference between adopted dogs/cats and those in pets shop ~

So, let's start an adoption for FREE !

Adoption & Purchase Procedures


Adoption Procedures Purchase Procedures
1. Click "Our Pets" to see the detailed pets' information. 1. Click "Our Supplies" to see the products gallery and corresponding prices.
2. After deciding which pet you want to adopt, click the "Start Adoption" button in the right-bottom corner. 2. After deciding what products you want to buy, click the "Start Purchase" button in the right-bottom corner.
3. You will be directed to the Adoption Form. Please fill in the information. 3. You will be directed to the Purchase Form. Please fill in the information.
4. After the submission, we will review your Form and
conduct a home survey based on the address you entered.
So please ensure the accuracy of the information in the Form.		 4. After the submission, we will contact you by phone and email if the ordered products are ready.
5. We will send the pet to you by hand if your application succeed. 5. The products will be sent to your delivery address and please make the cash paymnent in this step.
